About

My name's Victor Temprano, and I wanted to make something a bit more visual to help while learning about chemistry. Bohr diagrams are great, but 3D models are fun to play around with, and can really help us visualize what happens at a small scale (though imperfectly).

Current features:

  • Period 1, 2, and 3 elements and basic information
  • 3D animation that can be controlled/toggled
  • Illustrations of electron shells

In development:

  • Searchable periodic table
  • Normal periodic table layout
  • A few molecules (H20, acids, bases, ATP)
